About us...

We, Team Oblique, believe that everyone should get fair share in the society. We have seen that it is very difficult to get in touch with labourers for temporary services so, here We are with this platform which helps you to get directly in contact with these labourers for your domestic requirements. This App follows three simple steps i.e checking the availability of workers, booking them for specific time and finally, payment. One tap on the booking button, and we ensure that you have the premium services. Come! Join us in this venture...

I, Ankit Kumar , is a student of Vellore Institute of Technology, Bhopal, pursuing my degree in Computer Science .T he idea of such a platform struck to me when my house was being constructed and it was cumbersome job for my family to find the labourers for this work. This strenuous effort landed me to think over this problem and I decided to create an online platform which would make the search effortless as well as easy to hire the labourers as per the requirements of the customer. The Quarantine days proved to be a blessing in disguise for jotting down my ideas and publish. This is how the pillars of Oblique were established– One stop destination for all your domestic work requirements.
